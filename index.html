💡 Emergency Fund Rules
🏦
Where to Keep It
Liquid mutual fund, high-yield savings, or short-term FD. Never in equities or crypto.
🔒
Only Real Emergencies
Job loss, medical crisis, urgent repairs only — not vacations or planned purchases.
🔄
Rebuild After Using
Dip into it? Pause investments and rebuild back to 6 months before resuming.
📈
Liquid Funds > Savings
Liquid MFs yield ~6–7% with T+1 redemption — far better than 3–3.5% savings accounts.
